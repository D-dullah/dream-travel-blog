My Story

I grew up in the shadow of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest peak, surrounded by some of the most breathtaking landscapes on Earth. From the endless plains of the Serengeti to the turquoise waters of Zanzibar, Tanzania has been my playground, my classroom, and my inspiration.

As a self-taught web developer and lifelong explorer, I noticed something: most travel guides about Tanzania were written by outsiders who visited for a few weeks. While their perspectives are valuable, I wanted to create something different—a resource that shows the REAL Tanzania through local eyes.

DreamTravel was born from a simple idea: help people experience Tanzania the way locals do. Not just the famous tourist spots, but the hidden waterfalls, the local food markets, the secret viewpoints, and the cultural experiences that make Tanzania truly special.
